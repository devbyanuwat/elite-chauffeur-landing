# Impeccable — Elite Chauffeur Landing Page

This file defines the design context for `/impeccable` skills operating on the Elite Chauffeur (sabuygo.com) landing page. Read this before any design work on this project.

## Project

- **Product**: Elite Chauffeur — premium chauffeur / car-with-driver service in Bangkok, operated by Sabaigo (บริษัท OOM)
- **Surface**: Static marketing landing page (single `index.html` + `/images`, no build step, no framework)
- **Deploy**: Docker + nginx at sabuygo.com
- **Language**: Thai primary, English secondary (both must feel first-class — not a translation afterthought)
- **SEO baseline**: Current main scores 93/100; do not regress

## Design Context

### Users

The landing page is read by two audiences, often on the same visit:

1. **Thai executives and corporate assistants** booking car service for meetings, airport pickup, VIP transfers, and multi-day client hosting. They arrive on mobile during work hours, skim fast, and want to see: *are these people serious, can I hand my client to them, how do I book.*
2. **International business travelers and incoming tour operators** evaluating a Bangkok chauffeur partner. They arrive on desktop, compare against Blacklane / Wheely / hotel concierges, and need the page to signal professionalism without needing to read Thai.

The job-to-be-done is **trust transfer**: convert a cold visitor into someone who feels safe handing over their or their client's trip. Speed and reassurance matter far more than delight or novelty.

Primary contexts of use: mid-morning on a phone in a taxi, late-afternoon on a laptop between meetings, late-night when an urgent airport pickup is being arranged. The page must hold up under all three.

### Brand Personality

Three words: **composed, discreet, deliberate.**

Voice is the quiet side of luxury — the concierge who already knows your reservation, not the valet who shouts your name. It earns attention through precision instead of volume. Emotional goal: the visitor exhales slightly and thinks *"these people have done this before."* No hype, no urgency banners, no "book now get 20% off" — that language belongs to competitors we are deliberately not imitating.

### Aesthetic Direction

**Light mode, refined restraint.** Off-white background (not pure #fff), deep charcoal text (not pure #000), muted olive-gold as a rare, earned accent. The page should feel like a well-printed hotel dossier or the homepage of an Aman property — generous white space, left-aligned typography, text doing most of the work.

References (positive):
- **Aman Resorts, Alila, Rosewood** — silent confidence, sparse typography, photography that breathes, almost no decorative elements
- **Apple product pages** — precise spacing, confident restraint, photography as hero, type hierarchy driven by scale and weight rather than color

Anti-references (explicit — never resemble these):
- **Generic SaaS / Vercel-template look**: purple-to-blue gradients, three identical rounded cards in a row, glassmorphism everywhere, neon hero accents
- **Thai real-estate / รถเช่าประกัน 2015**: red-and-yellow stock flyers, shouty "โปรโมชั่น!" badges, mismatched icon sets, emoji in headings
- **Agency / awwwards showcase**: scroll-jacking, WebGL overkill, custom cursors, three-second intro loaders, motion for its own sake
- **Corporate-stiff consulting**: #003366 navy, handshake stock photo, "Excellence. Trust. Innovation." tagline, zero personality

Theme: **light** — off-white base (oklch ≈ 98% 0.005 85), charcoal body text (oklch ≈ 20% 0.01 85), muted gold accent refined from the current `#B8943F` toward something closer to oklch 65% 0.08 85 so it reads as *gilded* rather than *metallic yellow*. No pure black, no pure white, neutrals tinted subtly toward the gold hue for cohesion.

Typography direction: a **humanist / neo-grotesque Thai** that is *not* Noto Sans Thai, Kanit, Prompt, or Sarabun (the four Thai defaults the model will reach for first). Candidates to explore: Anuphan, Bai Jamjuree, Niramit, Charmonman-less alternatives from Cadson Demak — pick the one that sets *composed* rather than *friendly*. Pair it with a refined Latin sans from outside the reflex list (explore Pangram Pangram, Klim, Dinamo before settling — **do not use Inter, DM Sans, Plus Jakarta, Space Grotesk, IBM Plex, Outfit, or Instrument Sans**, all banned by impeccable's reflex list). The Latin and Thai faces must share similar x-height and weight feel — mixed-script headlines are common on this site.

Motion: **quiet.** Staggered fade + small translate on initial viewport reveal, restrained hover states (lift 1–2px, not 8px), no parallax, no scroll-linked effects beyond a fade-in threshold. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` completely. The page should feel *still and deliberate*, not *animated*.

### Design Principles

1. **Restraint over emphasis.** Every element must earn its place. If a section can be removed without losing meaning, remove it. More air, fewer containers.
2. **Typography carries hierarchy, not color.** Use weight, size, and spacing to rank importance. Accent color appears at most 2–3 times per viewport and only where a decision is being asked of the user (book, call, enquire).
3. **Muted gold is a guest, not a resident.** The accent stays rare. Never border-left stripes, never gradient fills on text, never tinted shadows. When in doubt, leave it off.
4. **Thai and English share one voice.** Both languages get the same hierarchy, spacing, and weight treatment. Thai must never look like it was bolted onto a Latin-first design.
5. **Fast, quiet, accessible by default.** Lighthouse 95+ on performance and accessibility is table stakes. No motion without a reduced-motion fallback. No color without contrast verification. The page should load as calmly as it looks.

## Notes for Implementation

- Current HTML uses inline `<style>` with Apple-style system (Inter + Noto Sans Thai, gold `#B8943F`). When `/impeccable craft` or related skills run, they may rewrite this stylesheet but must preserve all existing structured-data JSON-LD blocks and meta tags — SEO is load-bearing here.
- Images in `/images/` are the only real brand assets (logo.webp, car1–4.webp, service1–3.webp). Any new design must use these rather than inventing new hero imagery.
- `privacy.html` exists as a separate page and should inherit the same design system once it lands.
- Do not push or commit to `main` without an explicit instruction — Sabuygo redesign lives on `redesign/impeccable-v1` branch off main.
